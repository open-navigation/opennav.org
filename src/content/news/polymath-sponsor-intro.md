We're excited to introduce Polymath Robotics as a Nav2 sponsor!

![Polymath Robotics](/images/news/polymath-intro.png)

Polymath is on a mission to make autonomy accessible for any vehicle, and their support helps us continue developing Nav2 for the community.

## About Polymath Robotics

Polymath Robotics provides a platform that dramatically simplifies adding autonomy to vehicles. Their approach:

- **Reduces development time** from years to weeks
- **Works with existing vehicles** without major modifications
- **Handles the complexity** so you can focus on your application

## Why Polymath Supports Nav2

Polymath recognizes that a strong open-source foundation benefits everyone. By supporting Nav2, they're investing in:

- Continued maintenance and bug fixes
- New feature development
- Community support and documentation

## Looking Forward

We're excited to work with the Polymath team and explore how their platform and Nav2 can complement each other.

Thank you, Polymath, for believing in open-source robotics!

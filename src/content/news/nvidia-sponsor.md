We're proud to introduce NVIDIA as a Nav2 sponsor!

![NVIDIA Sponsor](/images/news/nvidia-sponsor.png)

NVIDIA has been at the forefront of accelerating robotics through their powerful GPUs and AI platforms. Their support for Nav2 represents a commitment to advancing open-source mobile robot navigation.

## NVIDIA and Robotics

NVIDIA's Isaac platform has become a cornerstone for robotics development, offering:

- **GPU-accelerated perception** for real-time sensor processing
- **Simulation environments** for safe testing and validation
- **AI inference** for intelligent navigation decisions

## What This Partnership Means

With NVIDIA's support, we can continue to optimize Nav2 for GPU acceleration, ensuring that teams can leverage the full power of modern hardware for their navigation needs.

Their expertise in parallel computing aligns perfectly with the performance demands of real-time robot navigation.

Thank you, NVIDIA, for supporting the Nav2 community!

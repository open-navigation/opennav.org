We're kicking off our sponsor introduction series with Dexory, our first Platinum-level sponsor!

![Dexory warehouse robot](/images/news/dexory.png)

## About Dexory

Founded in 2015 (formerly BotsAndUs), Dexory is a London-based company that recently raised a $19 Million Series A to expand globally. They develop robotics and AI solutions for warehouse operators, using their DexoryView platform to collect inventory data and create real-time digital representations of warehouse environments.

## Robot Capabilities

Their autonomous robot features impressive specifications:

- Can scan warehouse locations **up to 15 meters high**
- Processes **over 7,500 pallets per hour** during normal operation
- Operates even in **very narrow aisles (VNAs)**
- **24/7 operation** without disruption to warehouse activities

## Nav2 Integration

Dexory leverages several Nav2 navigation capabilities:

- **Smac Planner** (Hybrid-A*) for path planning in confined spaces
- **MPPI controller** for dynamic obstacle avoidance
- **Regulated Pure Pursuit** controller for task-specific tracking

The company uses custom behavior trees and Navigator plugins while maintaining Nav2's standard interfaces, avoiding the need to fork the core codebase.

## Why Sponsorship Matters

As a commercial user of Nav2, Dexory understands the value of contributing back to the open-source community. Their Platinum sponsorship helps ensure continued development, maintenance, and new features that benefit all users.

## Thank You, Dexory

We're grateful to have Dexory as our first Platinum sponsor and look forward to their continued involvement in the Nav2 community.

This is the first in our series of sponsor introductions. Stay tuned for more!
